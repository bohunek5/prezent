Sposób instalacji

1. Power supply - input 110/230 VAC (depending on power supply and electric supply)
- brown wire - L line
- blue wire - 0 neutral N
- green-yellow wire - ground, protective earth

2. Constant-voltage power supply

3. Power supply - LED output
- red wire - V+ LED (plus)
- black wire - V- LED (minus)

4, 5, 6. Ways of connecting LED bars to power supply:
- 4 - two-sided power supply - maximum current is dependent on design of bar and thickness of bar's paths, average about 5A (please remember about proper wire gauge)
- 5 - middle-point power supply - maximum current is dependent on design of bar and thickness of bar's paths, average about 5A (please remember about proper wire gauge)
- 5 - one-sided power supply - maximum current is dependent on design of bar and thickness of bar's paths, average about 3A (please remember about proper wire gauge)

Example from picture above:
Maximum current when powering three strips of LED bar 4,5,6 = 5A + 5A + 3A = 13A, and this gives us a power of:
P12V = (5A + 5A + 3A) x 12V = 60W + 60W + 36W = 156W while 12 V DC
P24V = (5A + 5A + 3A) x 24V = 120W + 120W + 72W = 312W while 24 V DC
