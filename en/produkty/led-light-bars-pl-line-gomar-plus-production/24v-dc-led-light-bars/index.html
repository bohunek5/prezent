24 V DC LED light bars

- PL line - professional product, produced by GOMAR PLUS
- high-quality electronic components - LEDs, PCB
- high luminous efficiency, long lifetime
- 24 V DC electronic circuit reaches higher luminous efficiency than 12 V DC products
- 24 V DC electronic circuit have twice as much current value compared to the 12 V circuit, which means lower voltage and brightness drops

With our own production line we can adapt products to customer demands including: power, luminous flux, CCT, CRI, dimensions etc.

LED light bars are produced to order, lead time is confirmed individually.
