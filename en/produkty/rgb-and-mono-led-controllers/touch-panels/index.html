Touch panels

LED touch panels

We offer LED touch panels. They'll make dimming, brightening or colour changing easy. You can also choose some effects, especially with RGB tapes. Panels can be mounted directly on wall with adhesive tape or in junction box, as normal switch. They are working with MONO and RGB LEDs, 12 - 24 V DC.

These panels don't require wiring, they work wirelessly with dedicated controllers

In our offer You'll find :

  • wall-mounted touch panels
  • panels for MONO LED
  • panels for CCT LED
  • panels for RGB LED
  • multifunction panels

Szanowni Państwo,
