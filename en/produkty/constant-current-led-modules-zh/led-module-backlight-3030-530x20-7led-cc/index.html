Basic info

- GOMAR PLUS production

- PCB - FR4 

- highest quality LEDs (OSRAM, CREE, SAMSUNG etc.)

- constant current power supply

- perfect light source for LED linear luminaires

- dimensions according to global standards

- high luminous flux, efficacy, lifetime, good colour repeatability

- our own SMD production line - we can customize all parameters according to customer demands

LED module 3030-530x20-7LED CC was designed as a backlight LED solution. With dedicated lenses we achieved a wide light beam (150 degrees) whereby you can get a plain backlight without visible LED dots.

Most important features:

- module can be split into 1LED sections

- designed for use with lenses

- backlight without LED dots, even lighting

- wide light beam
