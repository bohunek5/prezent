Basic info

Square-shaped downlight for recessed mounting. Luminaire is made of die-casting aluminium, and powder coated. There are three available optics version - 15, 24 nad 36 degrees. Light source can be customized considering luminous flux, power, colour temperature or CRI. SEDE lamps are available in three version - with one, two or three COBs.
