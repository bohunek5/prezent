Basic info

LED tape in transparent, sleeve cover ensuring IP67 protection degree. Product designed for demanding environment such us dusty or humid areas, like bathroom etc. Waterproof LED tapes are prepared to the length required by the customer, they're not intended for self-cutting. Cutting the tape results in a loss of airtightness.

  • PL LINE PRODUCT- PROFESSIONAL LINE, HIGHEST QUALITY, LUMINOSITY AND DURABILITY
  • PROFESSIONAL PCB MOUNTING, ONLY BRAND-NAME LED DIODES (OSRAM,CITIZEN,CREE ETC.)
  • FLEXIBLE LED TAPES, GOMAR PLUS PRODUCTION
  • EASY INSTALLATION, CAN BE MOUNTED ON UNEVEN SURFACES  

LED tape is available typically in power version: 4,8 | 9,6 | 14 W/m, and colour temperature: 3000K, 4000K, 5000K, CRI80.​

With our own production line we can customize LED tapes including parameters as:

- power per meter (W/m) 

- colour temperature: 2200K, 2400K, 2700K, 3500K, 5700K, 6500K

- LEDs colour: red, green, blue, amber etc.

- Colour Rendering Index (Ra): CRI90, CRI97

Customized products are priced individually. Please check exemplary variants of power and brigtness in chart below
