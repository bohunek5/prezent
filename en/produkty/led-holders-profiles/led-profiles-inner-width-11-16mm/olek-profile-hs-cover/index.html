Basic info

OLEK is designed for free-standing or suspended fixtures. With PDS-LUK cover you can obtain round luminaire. Smooth walls ensure modern aesthetics. For use in interior architecture in many configurations, thanks to the small lock, the profile can be conveniently used for constructing suspended, long, linear lighting fixtures.

 

 

Technical parameters

Material anodized aluminium
Length 1m, 2m, can be cutted on customer's order
Cover milky, transparent

Zastosowanie

- for flexible or hard LED strips
- lighting or illumination of certain areas (stream of light may vary depending on the power of applied LEDs)
- to build free standing lighting fixtures
- to be mounted as an individual element to surfaces (walls, furniture boards etc. ), not grooves milled in surfaces 
- indoor: interior design, scenography, advertisement, work stations (hardly accessible – shaded spots)

 

Cechy

Profile is, standardly sold with HS cover milky or transparent. Price - 9,60 netto/metre (included in profile's price) 

HS cover features:

- double-sided UV protections,

- good class of fire-resistance, 

- very good mechanical resistance, 

- high stiffness - it's good solution for fixtures, that are exposed to vibrations. Cover fit tightly in profile.  

HS covers were tested in tough weather conditions. Material - certified polycarbonate.

We offer also other models of covers, if you can't find proper one in accessories please contact us by e-mail.
