LED holders - profiles

As an official distributor of KLUŚ we can offer You a wide range of profiles and accessories for LED lighting. Profiles are designed for production of LED lamps, they are cover for light source and simplify mounting process. A wide range of accessories makes it easier to connect and assemble the luminaires. The availability of profiles in various shapes, sizes, and for various applications enables creation of lamps with various functionalities. Our profiles are equipped with transparent or milky covers. We are able to manufacture ready-made lamps to size or according to customer’s design, in any chosen profile and with any chosen LED light source. Light sources installed in the profiles are less susceptible to potential damage, and their use allows for reliable and long-lasting installation of lamps together with power supply and wiring.
