LED lamps manufacturing

We'll create LED lighting just, as You wish

LED bars and tapes from our offer can be prepared on customer's order according to specific demands. On order we product individual and nontypical products - electronic circuits, LED lamps, wiring etc. Thanks to our own production line for LED light sources we can realise projects made directly for customer's lighting applications. Moreover a wide range of profiles-holders makes easy to choose product with consideration of particular demands. In addition we provide photometric files (such as EULUMDAT, .ldt) useful for lighting designers. Beside customised indents we sell finished products.

On order we produce:

  • finished lamps of any configuration (lenght, type nad colour of profile-holder, power, luminosity and brightness of LEDs, wiring, end caps, mounting brackets)
  • lackering of profiles-holders, color can be chosen from NCS colours palette
